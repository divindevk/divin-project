introdution to st mary high school kiruhura

Saint Mary's High School Kiruhura is a mixed Catholic boarding school in Rwanda, founded in 1998 by the Sisters of St. Mary of Namur in the Huye district, Rusatira sector. It offers both O-Level and A-Level education, focusing on academic disciplines like PCM, PCB, and MPG, while fostering culture and strong community values.

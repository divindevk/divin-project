historical background of st mary high school kiruhura

Kiruhura Secondary School is a school founded by the Sisters of Saint Mary of Namur. It opened in 1971, being a school family to meet the needs of the surrounding population and support the young Rwandan girl. In 1978, when family schools were closed, the sisters of St. Mary of Namur held immediately a Rural Learning and integrated handwork Center. The center operated until the 1994 genocide of Tutsi. After 1994, the school started again at the ordinary level and subsequently opened the advanced level with the sciences option. Today, the school has the following combination for the advance level: Maths-Physics & Geography, with the expectation to open the next academic year a new combination of Math-Physics-Chemistry.

introduction to admissions

St. Mary's High School Kiruhura, located in Rwanda, offers secondary education with programs including PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology). Admissions generally involve submitting application documents and meeting with school administration, with updates occasionally posted via their official Instagram page.
